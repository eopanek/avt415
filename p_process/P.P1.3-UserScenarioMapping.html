P. P1. 3: User scenario mapping

Actor

Bobo

Motivator

Poor sleeping posture and restless nights

Intention

Buy a body pillow that encases the body fully to get a better night's sleep

Action

Bobo searches through pillow websites in search of a body pillow with both good reviews and an enclosing design.

Resolution

Bobo finds the suitable pillow on the website after filtering product results and buys the body pillow.

Scenario

Bobo has been having trouble falling asleep lately and thinks it might be due to her choice of pillows. Bobo decides to look for a body pillow that will encase her body and has a lot of positive feedback and ratings. After finding the company “Fort P's”, she goes through their body pillow selection and decides to buy the body pillow that she finds after filtering through the products.
